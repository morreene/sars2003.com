SARS 2003

a collection of data and files for research

The 2003 outbreak of SARS, caused by the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus (SARS-CoV or SARS-CoV-1), originated in China and primarily affected the region. This website serves as a repository for the data published by Chinese authorities, providing the most detailed and granular information available for research and analysis. In addition to data, the site also collects news, articles, and papers documenting this significant public health crisis.
